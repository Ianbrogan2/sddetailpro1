Our Services

Top-notch car care at affordable prices

At SD Detail Pro, we offer two premium services tailored to meet your car's needs:

Basic Car Wash - $30

Our Basic Car Wash is a thorough exterior cleaning designed to remove dirt and grime, leaving your car looking fresh and polished. It's perfect for regular maintenance and will keep your vehicle shining between detailed cleanings.

Full Car Detailing - $60

For a comprehensive clean that goes beyond the basics, choose our Full Car Detailing service. This premium option includes an in-depth interior and exterior treatment, ensuring every inch of your car is meticulously cleaned and protected. Ideal for a complete refresh or special occasions, it delivers the highest standard of care and attention to detail.
